Protein, Fat, and Carbs — By The Numbers

A calorie target without macros is just a guess

Example cutting target: 1,791 cal · 160 lb · moderate deficit

The allocation order
Protein first → fat % of calories → carbs fill the rest
01

Protein (grams per lb)

Set by body weight — ~1g/lb during a cut to help preserve lean mass (example: 160g)

02

Fat (% of calories)

Fixed share for hormones and satiety — typically ~25% of total calories (example: 50g)

03

Carbs (whatever remains)

Flex with energy needs after protein and fat are set (example: 176g)

04

Treat as starting points

Track 2–3 weeks, then adjust based on scale trend, gym performance, and how you feel
